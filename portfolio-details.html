Portfolio Details

This is a website developed for YACOT (Youth Action for Community Transformation)

YACOT Website Project

Youth Action for Community Transformation is a non-profit making, refugee youth led organization founded in 2020 to promote peaceful coexistence, self-reliant and access to fair and equal access to health services among refugee and host community in Rhino Camp Refugee Settlement.

YACOT was founded in May 2020 by a group of like minded South Sudanese refugee youth who have experienced violent conflicts and deep divisions back in South Sudan and again within Rhino Camp Refugee Settlement, and the founders were perturbed by the increasing cases of teenage pregnancy in the settlement, and decreasing livelihood which breads conflicts due to scarcity of these services

This website project was aimed at reaching out and transforming the community members. More than 30 refugees had their lives changed this initiative

Project Overview

The website for Youth Action for Community Transformation (YACOT) presents a clear and engaging organizational profile that highlights its mission to promote health education, peacebuilding, and community empowerment for youth, women, and vulnerable groups. It outlines YACOT’s core values, programs, target groups, and the context behind its founding, emphasizing inclusive community transformation and conflict resolution

The site also provides structured navigation to key sections such as About Us, Programs, Partnerships, Publications, and Contact/Donate options, enabling visitors to easily access information and engage with the organization’s work. It is designed to inform stakeholders, partners, and the general public about YACOT’s background, vision, and ongoing community impact.
